Haiku is a restaurant offering Japanese food to college-aged students who want sushi near campus. There is also a second location in the Mendota Heights area, but it is most known for its food in the Minneapolis-Washington Avenue area. Unlike competing sites, which are far from campus or expensive, Haiku will provide affordable, high-quality food nearby.
